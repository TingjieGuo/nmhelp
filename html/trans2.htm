 MEANING: Choice of Translator Routine for PREDPP
 CONTEXT: Option of NM-TRAN $SUBROUTINES record

 USAGE:
 $SUBROUTINES [TRANS=]TRANS2

 SAMPLE:
 $SUBROUTINE ADVAN1 TRANS2

 DISCUSSION:
 TRANS2  is a routine in PREDPP's library which can be used with ADVAN1
 or ADVAN2.  It performs a reparameterization of the basic  PK  parame-
 ters to the internal parameters K and KA.

 Basic PK parameters for ADVAN1, TRANS2:

      CL   clearance
      V    volume of distribution

      Relationship: K=CL/V

 Basic PK parameters for ADVAN2, TRANS2:

      CL   clearance
      V    volume of distribution
      KA   rate constant of absorption

      Relationship: K=CL/V
      KA=KA

REFERENCES: Guide VI Section III.A, VII.C.1
